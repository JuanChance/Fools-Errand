About Me

Hey, my name's Vince Palban, sometimes I go by Fool's Errand.

I'm a 2nd Year Creative Digital Media student in Technological University Dublin, Blanchardstown. I have a deep interest in both traditional and digital art and hope to enventually persue some dort of career in the art or design fields. In terms of digital art I am most interested in Adobe Illustrator and, very recently, voxel modelling and for traditional I am interested in drawing and craft/3D modelling, among several others.

On this website you'll find a few examples of some of my work but you can also check my social media pages for more examples.

I am fully open for commission, you can contact me either through this website or through social media.
